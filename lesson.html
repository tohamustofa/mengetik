PinterNgetik was created out of dissatisfaction at not being able to locate a free online typing exam that had all of the characteristics that were thought to be essential for any typing test. We have created free typing courses locally for people of all ages, backgrounds, and ability levels to learn how to type. includes a game, a free typing test, assistance, and video instructions. Please feel free to get in touch with us if you have any questions or concerns, would like a certain feature to be included, or if you run into any issues.
